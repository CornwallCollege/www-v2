Undergraduate Courses with The Cornwall College Group

There's never been a better time to gain a quality degree right here in Cornwall, with an incredible range of over 80 programmes providing you with real skills to start the career you want straight away.

Accommodation

We understand that finding the right place to live is just as important as finding the right course to study. Here at the Cornwall College Group we have a list of accommodation around the local areas of each of our campuses that are available to you.

Find out more

Our Courses

All programmes are developed with industry, giving you the chance to work on live projects, so you start your career before you graduate.

The diversity of our curriculum means you could study surf science on a beach, zoology at Newquay Zoo, or marine science in Falmouth. We are also introducing new three-year honours degree programmes every year.

View our courses

Money

If you are planning to join us in 2015 and live in the EU you will be glad to know that our fees are set from £7,500 per year. A range of scholarships and bursaries are available.
