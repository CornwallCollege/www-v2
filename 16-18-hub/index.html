Our specialist programmes of study combine qualifications, work readiness skills training and work experience, giving you a careers edge and the very best preparation for the world of work.

We offer a variety of qualifications including A-levels, BTEC and City & Guilds Diplomas, Technical Baccalaureates, Practical Skills Training, NVQs and Apprenticeships at our colleges: Cornwall College, Duchy College, Bicton College and Falmouth Marine School.
