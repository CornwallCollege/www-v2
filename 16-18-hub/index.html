Our specialist programmes of study combine qualifications, work readiness skills training and work experience, giving you a careers edge and the very best preparation for the world of work.

We offer a variety of qualifications including A-levels, BTEC and City & Guilds Diplomas, Technical Baccalaureates, Practical Skills Training, NVQs and Apprenticeships at our colleges: Cornwall College, Duchy College, Bicton College and Falmouth Marine School.

A few reasons to choose #thecareercollege… (this may be punch boxes in a slider perhaps? Dunno but needs to be SEO searchable) We are one of the best colleges for catering studies in the UK, with partnerships with Nathan Outlaw, River Cottage and Fifteen Cornwall.

We are the biggest provider of apprenticeships in the South West, with over 80 career pathways to choose from. We are at the forefront of delivering cutting edge training that’s right for industry, our partnership with Software Cornwall has seen the emergence of a new tech Bacc in Software Development, delivered for the first time in industry at Bluefruit Software. Our students and staff are award-winners, they include champion SkillBuild trades apprentices, Film School and media students winning over 30 awards in a decade and our Duchy College is the only land-based college to win a Queen’s Anniversary Prize for Education & Training.

We work with many of the regions big industry leaders to ensure our learners are ready for the workplace:

  • Pendennis Shipyards
  • Blue Flame Academy
  • Kier Academy
  • Mill Auto Supplies
  • NHS
  • Plymouth Argyle FC

Our learners go on to great things: for example 100% of our watersports students are employed by the end of their course and travel the world.
