Equine sector jobs allow you to follow a career working with something you are passionate about: horses. Whether that's the welfare, husbandry and supervision of horses in a stable or livery, training thoroughbred racehorses or coaching and breeding. You could be a jockey, showjumper or support technician, there is a wide range of careers to choose from.
