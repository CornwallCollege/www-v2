Wi-fi changes

During October half-term we launched a new wi-fi service called eduroam. The old cc-open service will be retired during the Christmas break.

The benefits are:

  • one-time only authentication (until your password needs changing)
  • improved performance and functionality
  • greater compatibility with smart phones, tablets and consoles
  • increased security
  • a future update will allow you to login from any worldwide educational establishment participating in the eduroam wi-fi service

How to connect your device:

  • Forget or remove your connection to CC-Open
  • Connect to eduroam
  • Your device will prompt for your username:
    • For students enter your username with @live.cornwall.ac.uk at the end of it e.g. joe.bloggs123@live.cornwall.ac.uk
    • For staff enter your username with @cornwall.ac.uk at the end of it e.g. joe.bloggs@cornwall.ac.uk - regardless of what your email address is
  • You may get prompted to accept a security certificate - radius.cornwall.ac.uk issued by QuoVadis Root CA 2. Accept this.
  • Your device will remain connected until your college password expires, or you change it, in which case you must update the saved password on your device.
