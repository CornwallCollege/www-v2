Students are able to take part in free and subsidised sporting and recreational activities designed to enrich the student experience whilst at the College. Our College Sportsmaker arranges a varied timetable of sports activities each term for those interested.
There is a wide range of facilities including a 3G astro turf, 4 court sports hall,football/rugby pitches, climbing tower, low ropes course, lake for watersports, cross country running courses, teaching rooms and a fitness suite

College sporting activities include; Hockey, Netball, Football, Kayaking, Outdoor, pursuits, Horse riding, Climbing, Running, Fitness Classes, Archery, Lacrosse, Racket Sports, Dodgeball and many more
