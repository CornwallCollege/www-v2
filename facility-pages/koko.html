We only use high-quality professional products, from brands like Dermalogica, St Tropez, Goldwell, Moroccan Oil and CND. Retail sizes are available to purchase from all Koko salons.

We offer skin rejuvenation and hair removal with the Lynton Luminette Advance Intense Pulsed Light System (IPL). Contact your nearest Koko salon to check availability and discuss your treatment requirements.

The department for Hair, Beauty & Wellbeing has gone from strength to strength in recent years.

Koko Salon is open to the public. There is dedicated customer parking available.
