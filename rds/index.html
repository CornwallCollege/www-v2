For full instructions on how to connect from Windows computers, Apple OSx, tablets and smartphones please see the full guide on the intranet, or email helpdesk@cornwall.ac.uk for further assistance.
N.B. If you receive the following message, you will need to change your password via the PWD website, instructions below.


Change Password Instructions

Please go to https://rdpbroker.cornwall.ac.uk/RDWeb/Pages/en-US/password.aspx

You will be presented with the following screen:



In the account box, enter ccg\username (e.g. ccg\sara.opie)
In the old password box, enter your old password
Enter your new password twice*

Click OK.

You should get a message saying password successfully changed.

*Your new password must meet complexity and length requirements - it must be at least 8 characters long and must contain at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter and one number. It must not contain any part of your name.
